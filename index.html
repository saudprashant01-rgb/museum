Preserving Our Cultural Legacy

Mahendranagar Museum stands as a guardian of the rich cultural heritage and untold stories of Far-Western Nepal.

Our regional museum offers a quiet and educational space showcasing traditional tools, vibrant clothing, and historical artifacts that define our roots. With a unique outdoor aircraft exhibit, it's an authentic gateway for curious travelers, students, and families eager to discover local history.
